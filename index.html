Objectives

Our goal is to implement the following extensions to our current path-tracer implementation:

  • Realtime Rendering
  • Volumetric Lighting (implementation structure from insert link here)
  • Glass (refractive BRDFs)
  • compatibility with .obj files

To demonstrate these features, we will be attempting to render a car in foggy conditions.

The volumetric lighting features will render the exhaust gases while a glass shader will render the windows. Furthermore, we need to add .obj file compatibility to render the car model.

What we have so far:

Following volumetric path-tracing write-ups from UCSD Graduate School Classes, we've achieved very primitive volumetric lighting. This currently only includes the rendering of monochromatic absorption-only homogeneous volumes.

Our next immediate goal is to model the scattering of light from other emissive sources into other volumes.
